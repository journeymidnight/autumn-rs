Main gaps (tracked in spec §10 as P3+ follow-ups):
  - UcxConn::Drop currently leaks the EP rather than calling
    ucp_ep_close_nbx, because UCX close synchronously cancels in-flight
    ops on the peer's side and races test patterns that drop
    immediately after writing. Production EPs are pooled in
    autumn-rpc/autumn-stream ConnPool and never dropped in steady
    state. Real fix needs ucp_worker_flush + await before destroy.
  - Eventfd-driven progress (ucp_worker_arm + AsyncFd) deferred —
    chicken-and-egg vs single-thread mode means per-runtime spawned
    polling task (50µs sleep) is the working model. Phase 5 perf
    measurement will show whether 50µs overhead matters.
