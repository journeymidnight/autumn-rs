  New file:
    crates/common/src/cpu_pin.rs
      - `available_cpu_cores() -> &'static [usize]` (cached in OnceLock,
        sorted ascending) snapshots the process cpuset on first call.
      - `pick_cpu_for_ord(zero_based_ord) -> Option<usize>` returns the

        cpuset.
